About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the System Software Laboratory at UNIST, advised by Young-ri Choi. I work on data management systems, making them faster and more efficient by exploiting the characteristics of the data and workloads they serve. So far, that has meant index structures for data management, along with caching and storage techniques for key-value stores. Going forward, I want to bring the same data- and workload-aware lens to a broader range of emerging data-intensive systems.
